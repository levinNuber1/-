No Message Moving or Copying

Messages are fixed within the chat, making it impossible to move or copy them. This prevents content from being extracted outside the chat environment, keeping your discussions private and contained.

Screenshot Blocking

Screenshot functionality is restricted within the chat, stopping users from capturing chat content and further safeguarding sensitive information. Your privacy is protected, even from internal threats.

End-to-End Encryption

All messages are encrypted, ensuring that only you and the recipient can read them. Mute cannot read your messages, and neither can anyone else.

Commands Guide

//clear

Clears all messages in the chat window. Use this command to instantly remove your chat history, adding an extra layer of control over your privacy.

number/

(e.g., Hello 5/): Sets a countdown timer for a message, making it disappear after that many seconds. Great for sharing sensitive information that only needs to be seen briefly.

//help

Displays a list of all available commands. Use this if you ever need a reminder of what Mute's commands can do for you.

//lock

Locks the chat window, preventing any new messages from being sent or received until unlocked. Useful for maintaining focus and security.
